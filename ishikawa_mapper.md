# [QA-DMAIC-001] Ishikawa Diagram: Weaving Section Downtime

### 🐟 Root Cause Categorization

**1. Machine (Maquinaria)**
- SOMET/VAMATEX mechanical wear.
- Critical failures in Loom 1 and Loom 4.
- High downtime rates (up to 39.8%).

**2. Material (Materiales)**
- High frequency of warp breakage.
- Quality of yarn affecting continuity.

**3. Method (Métodos)**
- Setup inconsistencies across different shifts.
- Lack of standardized preventive maintenance for critical units.

**4. Measurement (Medición)**
- Accuracy of downtime records per machine.
- Comparison of quality costs vs. sales revenue.
